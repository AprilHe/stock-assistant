# Breakout Strategy

## Core Idea
Buy strength when price clears a recent resistance range with confirmation from trend and participation.

## Typical Entry
- Price closes above prior 20-day high.
- Structure remains bullish (`close > SMA20 > SMA50`).
- Volume is above baseline, not drying up.

## Typical Exit
- Close falls back below short trend support (for example SMA20).
- Stop loss triggers or setup invalidates.
- Momentum fails to continue after several sessions.

## Main Risks
- False breakouts in choppy markets.
- Late entries after overextended moves.
- Thin volume on breakout day.
