# Commodity Macro Strategy

## Core Idea
Trade commodity direction when price trend and macro regime point in the same direction.

## Typical Inputs
- Commodity trend state (20/50-day structure, momentum).
- Dollar trend and rate moves.
- Risk-on or risk-off equity backdrop.

## Typical Exit
- Price closes below short trend support.
- Macro tailwinds weaken or reverse.
- Volatility rises beyond acceptable range.

## Main Risks
- Sudden macro headline reversals.
- Correlation breakdown across assets.
- Elevated event risk around policy data.
