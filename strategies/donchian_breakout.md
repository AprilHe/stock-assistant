# Donchian Breakout Strategy

## Core Idea
Capture directional expansions by entering new highs and exiting on channel breakdown.

## Typical Entry
- Close above 20-day Donchian high
- Better quality when volume confirms

## Typical Exit
- Close below 10-day Donchian low
- Optional stop-loss or time-based exit overlays

## Main Risks
- Whipsaw in range-bound markets
- False breakouts with weak participation
- Fast reversals after news shocks
