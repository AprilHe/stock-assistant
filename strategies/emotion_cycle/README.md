# Emotion Cycle Strategy

## What It Is
This strategy maps market behavior to crowd psychology phases and uses contrarian entries near emotional extremes.

## Typical Setup
- `Buy-leaning`: fear/panic narrative dominates, but selling pressure starts to fade and price stabilizes.
- `Sell-leaning`: euphoria narrative dominates, while extension/momentum looks exhausted.
- Always require at least one confirmation from price action before acting.

## Why Use It
- Helps avoid chasing obvious headlines.
- Adds a repeatable framework when sentiment gets one-sided.
- Works as an overlay to trend or breakout systems.

## Main Risks
- Emotion signals alone can be early.
- Strong trends can stay irrational longer than expected.
- News shocks can invalidate the setup quickly.

## Operational Notes
- Keep stop placement objective (recent structure invalidation).
- Use smaller size during high volatility or major event windows.
- Reassess thesis daily; exit when narrative normalizes.
