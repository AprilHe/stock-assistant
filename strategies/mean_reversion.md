# Mean Reversion Strategy

## Core Idea
Buy statistically stretched downside moves and target reversion back toward the mean.

## Typical Entry
- Price below lower Bollinger band (20,2)
- RSI(14) oversold
- No clear structural collapse signal

## Typical Exit
- Price returns toward SMA20
- RSI rebounds into neutral
- Stop out when downside trend accelerates

## Main Risks
- Falling-knife behavior in strong downtrends
- News-driven gap risk
- Volatility expansion can delay reversion
