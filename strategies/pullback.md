# Pullback Strategy

## Core Idea
In strong uptrends, buy temporary weakness near dynamic support instead of chasing highs.

## Typical Entry
- Higher-level trend remains bullish.
- Price pulls back towards SMA20 while holding key structure.
- Volume profile during pullback is neutral or contracting.

## Typical Exit
- Trend fails (for example close below SMA50).
- Invalidation level breaks.
- Price rebounds into target zone and momentum fades.

## Main Risks
- Pullback can become full trend reversal.
- Volatile tapes can overshoot support.
- Weak market regime reduces bounce probability.
