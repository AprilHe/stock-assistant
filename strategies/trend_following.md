# Trend Following Strategy

## Core Idea
Participate in persistent directional moves and hold while trend structure remains intact.

## Typical Entry
- `close > SMA50`
- `SMA20 > SMA50`
- Positive medium-term slope

## Typical Exit
- `close < SMA50`
- Short trend breaks down versus medium trend

## Main Risks
- Late entries after an extended run
- Whipsaws in sideways markets
- Regime shifts around major macro events
